ABOUT

Consious Coffee was founded in 2017 by two award winning baristas and fine taste loving enthusiasts. On a mission to share their love for high quality, clean and delicate tastes in coffees, teas and sweets. Putting their hearts into their craft and business. Taking responisibility for the environmental and societal footprints their business makes. Using only fairtrade and locally produced products, making it part of their trademark.

Alot of thought and soul is poured in each cup of coffee and slice of cake.
